ESTRUTURA INICIAL DO HTML:


        <!DOCTYPE html>
    <html lang="pt-br">
    <head>
        <meta charset="UTF-8">
        <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
        <title>Título do site</title>
    </head>
    <body>
    </body>
    </html>
